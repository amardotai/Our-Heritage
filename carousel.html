OUR HERITAGE
KASHI
Kashi, often called Varanasi, is a sacred city in India situated on the banks of the Ganges River. Revered in Hinduism, it's a pilgrimage site and cultural hub, known for its ghats, temples, and bustling markets. Kashi is steeped in spirituality and history, attracting visitors seeking enlightenment and cultural immersion.
